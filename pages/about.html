About us

Features

The features of the music app are-
• Mood based music app .
• Music app with the feature of immersive slide show.
• Embedded virtual piano in the app .
• Embedded drum-kit in the app.

How to use the app

The user will be directed to the pages of various moods where a mood is selected or the user can also select either to play a virtual piano or drum-kit. Then after selecting the mood , the user will be choosing the language based on which the songs will be displayed. The user will also be getting the feature to play the song from the local storage.
